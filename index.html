ABOUT ME

My name is Modther Soliman Hassan. I'm a Computer Science student at Cairo University — but I've never waited for graduation to start building.

What drives me most is projects that have a distinct artistic and directorial identity — videos that don't just deliver information, but leave a feeling. I'm drawn to work that challenges the way a story is told: the pacing, the mood, the moment a cut hits just right.

Video editing is a genuine passion for me — not just a skill. Even as I build my path in cybersecurity, this craft stays with me because I love it, not because I have to. That passion shows in every project I take on.

I started volunteering at CIBF 54 unsure if my skills were good enough. Four years later, I was leading an entire video editors team as Media Leader at CIBF 57. I completed the McKinsey Forward Program — because even my soft skills aren't left to chance.
